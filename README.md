## 🧭 What is Inventra?

**Inventra** is a **real-time inventory management platform** designed to help businesses efficiently track stock, manage products, and streamline operations.

It eliminates manual tracking by providing a **centralized system** for inventory visibility and control.

---

## ⚡ Core Idea

> 💡 *“Know what you have, where it is, and when you need it — instantly.”*

Inventra helps businesses:
- Avoid stockouts ❌  
- Prevent overstocking 📉  
- Improve operational efficiency ⚙️  

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 📦 Inventory Management
- Add, update, and delete products  
- Track stock levels in real-time  
- Categorize products  

### 🔔 Smart Alerts
- Low stock notifications  
- Inventory threshold tracking  

### 📊 Dashboard & Insights
- Overview of total inventory  
- Stock trends and usage patterns  
- Quick business insights  

### 🧾 Order & Stock Handling
- Incoming and outgoing stock tracking  
- Order history management  

### 👥 Role-Based Access
- Admin and staff roles  
- Controlled permissions
